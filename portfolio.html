✨ Welcome ✨

I am the type of person who enjoys serenity; thus, I prefer writing to speaking. Throughout my semesters at school, I have been assigned to produce various types of writing in multiple classes. Not only does writing help justify ideas clearly, but it also supports the development of critical thinking and reasoning skills. Although I tend to focus on logic and precision, this course pushed me to think more about my audience and how information is presented. Instead of only explaining concepts, I had to consider how different readers would interpret my writing. This became especially important in my major research project, where I explored the role of machine learning in the data science discourse community

In that project, I collected and analyzed over 600 posts from the Stack Exchange Data Science forum using text analysis and visualization in RStudio. This project also challenged me not only to present technical information but also to make it accessible and meaningful to a broader audience.
